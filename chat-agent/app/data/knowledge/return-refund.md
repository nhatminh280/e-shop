# Return and Refund Policy

This policy explains how to return items, request a refund, and track refund status. It applies to all standard purchases on our online store. By placing an order, you accept the terms below.

## Return Window

You can request a return within 30 days of receiving your order. The window starts on the delivery date recorded by the carrier. Requests outside this window are declined automatically and the item is not eligible for refund or store credit.

We extend the window to 45 days for orders placed between December 1 and December 24, to account for holiday gift-giving. Items in that window remain eligible for return until January 31 of the following year.

## Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible, the item must meet all of the following:

- Unworn, unwashed, and free of stains, odors, or pet hair.
- All original tags still attached.
- Original packaging included where applicable, such as shoe boxes or accessory pouches.
- A receipt, order confirmation email, or order number available for verification.

Items that fail these conditions are rejected at the warehouse and returned to the customer at the customer's expense.

## Exclusions

The following items are not eligible for return:

- Items marked as final sale on the product page or order confirmation.
- Gift cards and store credit.
- Underwear, swimwear inner liners, and personal care items, for hygiene reasons.
- Items damaged by misuse, accident, or wear inconsistent with normal use.
- Items modified, altered, or repaired outside our official service program.

If you believe an exclusion was applied incorrectly, contact support and include your order number.

## How to Start a Return

1. Sign in to your account and open your order history.
2. Select the order containing the item you want to return.
3. Choose the item and select Start Return.
4. Pick a reason from the dropdown and confirm the return method.
5. Print the prepaid return label emailed to you.
6. Pack the item securely and drop the package at a participating carrier location within 14 days of starting the return.

If you checked out as a guest, use the order lookup page with your order number and email to start the return.

## Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout. We cannot redirect refunds to a different card or account. If the original method has expired or been closed, contact support so we can arrange store credit instead.

For gift card orders, the refund returns to the gift card balance. For mixed payments, we refund the gift card first and then the remaining balance to the credit card.

## Refund Timing

Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected. Your bank or card issuer may take an additional 3 to 10 business days to post the refund to your statement. We do not control bank-side processing times.

You receive an email when the refund is initiated and another when it is fully processed. The order history page shows refund status in real time.

## Exchanges

We do not process direct exchanges. To exchange an item, return the original for a refund and place a new order for the replacement size, color, or product. This keeps inventory accurate and prevents your replacement from going out of stock.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If an item arrives damaged or defective, contact support within 7 days of delivery. Include your order number, a description of the issue, and photographs that show the damage clearly. We arrange a free return label and either refund or ship a replacement at no cost, subject to availability.

## Store Credit

You can choose store credit instead of a refund when starting the return. Store credit is issued within one business day of approval and never expires. Store credit balances are visible on your account page and can be applied at checkout.

Store credit is non-transferable and cannot be converted back to a cash refund once issued. If you change your mind within 14 days of issuance, contact support and we will reverse the credit and issue a refund to your original payment method instead, provided the credit has not yet been spent.

## Partial Returns

If your order contained multiple items and you only want to return some of them, start a return for the specific items only. The remaining items in the order are unaffected and the order summary updates to show the partial refund. Discounts that were applied to the full order are pro-rated across the returned items, so the refunded amount may be less than the line item price shown at checkout.

## International Returns

For international orders, the return window is 30 days. The customer is responsible for return shipping costs and any duties or taxes incurred during the return. Use a tracked shipping method, since we cannot refund items lost in transit without proof of delivery.

## Questions

For questions about a return or refund, contact support through the help center, include your order number, and select the Returns category. Our team responds within one business day.
