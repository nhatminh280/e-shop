# Shipping Policy

This policy describes how we ship orders, the delivery times we offer, applicable fees, supported areas, and how to track a shipment. It applies to all standard purchases on our online store.

## Service Areas

We ship to all 63 provinces and cities in Vietnam. We also ship to selected international destinations in Southeast Asia, including Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, the Philippines, and Indonesia. International availability is shown at checkout based on the destination address you enter.

We do not currently ship to PO boxes, military addresses, or freight-forwarder warehouses. Orders to these addresses are cancelled and refunded automatically within one business day.

## Delivery Methods

We offer three delivery methods. The methods available at checkout depend on the destination, the items in your cart, and current carrier capacity.

- **Standard.** 3 to 5 business days within Vietnam. Default for most domestic orders.
- **Express.** 1 to 2 business days within major cities, including Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, and Da Nang.
- **International.** 7 to 14 business days, depending on destination and customs clearance.

Business days exclude Saturdays, Sundays, and Vietnamese public holidays. Orders placed on a non-business day enter processing on the next business day.

## Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1 business day after payment is confirmed. Orders placed before 14:00 local time are usually processed the same day. Orders placed after 14:00 are processed the next business day.

If an item in your order requires extended processing, the product page and the order confirmation will show an updated lead time. Backorder lead times do not count against the standard delivery window.

## Shipping Fees

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on the destination, weight, and selected delivery method. The fee appears on the order summary before payment.

- **Standard domestic.** 30,000 VND flat for orders under 1,000,000 VND.
- **Free standard domestic.** Orders of 1,000,000 VND or more, after discounts.
- **Express domestic.** 60,000 VND flat, regardless of cart total.
- **International.** Quoted at checkout based on destination and weight.

Promotional shipping offers, such as free express during a campaign, override these defaults when applicable.

## Tracking

You receive a tracking number by email when your order ships. The tracking number is also visible on your order details page. Tracking events are typically updated within 4 to 6 hours of a carrier scan.

If tracking has not updated for more than 48 hours, contact support and include the order number. We escalate to the carrier on your behalf and respond within one business day.

## Failed Delivery and Address Changes

Carriers attempt delivery up to two times. If both attempts fail and the package is returned to our warehouse, we contact you to confirm a corrected address. A reshipment fee may apply.

To change a delivery address before the order ships, sign in, open the order, and select Edit Address. Once an order has shipped, the address cannot be changed through self-service. Contact support and we will request rerouting from the carrier where possible.

## Customs, Duties, and Taxes

For international orders, the recipient is responsible for any duties, taxes, or import fees charged by the destination country. We do not collect these at checkout. Customs delays are not counted toward our quoted delivery window.

## Lost or Damaged Shipments

If your tracking shows delivered but you have not received the package, check with neighbors and your building's reception, then contact support within 7 days of the delivery scan. We open a carrier investigation, which typically resolves within 5 to 10 business days.

If a package arrives visibly damaged, take photographs of the outer packaging before opening, document the damage to any items inside, and contact support within 7 days of delivery. We arrange a free return or a replacement at no cost, subject to inventory availability.

## Split Shipments

Orders containing items from multiple warehouses may ship in separate parcels. Each parcel has its own tracking number, and your order details page shows the status of each. You are not charged extra shipping for split shipments.

## Holiday Periods

During Vietnamese New Year (Tet) and selected international holidays, processing and delivery times may extend by up to 5 business days. We post a banner on the home page and inside the help center two weeks before each affected period so you can plan ahead.

For Tet specifically, the warehouse pauses processing during the official holiday week and resumes the next business day after the public holiday ends. Orders placed during the pause are queued in order of payment confirmation and processed first-in-first-out when work resumes. Tracking remains live, so you can monitor your package once it ships.

## Pickup Points

Some destinations support carrier pickup points as an alternative to home delivery. At checkout, choose Pickup Point and select a participating location near you. The carrier holds the package at the pickup point for up to 7 days, after which it is returned to our warehouse and the order moves to refund processing.

## Questions

For shipping questions not answered here, contact support through the help center, include your order number, and select the Shipping category. Our team responds within one business day.
