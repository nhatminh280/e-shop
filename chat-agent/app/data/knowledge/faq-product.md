# Product FAQ

This FAQ covers the most common questions about products, stock availability, color and size variants, and how to be notified when an item comes back in stock. For sizing details, see the Size Guide. For ordering and shipment questions, see the Order FAQ.

## How do I check stock availability?

Open any product page. Each color and size combination shows availability inline:

- **In Stock.** Available to ship today and add to cart.
- **Low Stock.** Fewer than five units remain. Add to cart promptly to secure the item.
- **Out of Stock.** Currently unavailable. The Notify Me button is shown in place of Add to Cart.

Stock counts shown on the product page are refreshed every few minutes. If stock changes between page load and adding to cart, we display a clear message and adjust your cart automatically.

## Why does the same product have multiple variants?

A product is a family of variants that share design, materials, and category. Variants differ by attributes like color, size, or fit. The product page lets you choose attributes, and the price, stock, and image set update to reflect the selected variant.

Some product pages also surface related products that share a design language but are independent items, such as a matching jacket and pants. Each related product has its own page and its own stock.

## How do I choose a color?

Open the product page and select a color swatch in the Color row. The image carousel switches to that color and the size availability updates. If a color is greyed out, it is out of stock in every size; consider Notify Me to receive an email when any size returns.

The displayed color is rendered to match in-store lighting. Screen calibration varies, so the actual fabric color may differ slightly. We accept returns under the standard return policy if the color does not meet your expectation.

## How do I choose a size?

Open the product page and select a size in the Size row. Greyed-out sizes are out of stock in the chosen color. To check availability across colors quickly, change the color and watch the size row update.

If you need help with sizing, open the Size Guide linked on the product page. The guide covers measuring, reading the chart, and choosing between sizes.

## Can I get notified when an out-of-stock item returns?

Yes. Open the product page, select the color and size you want, and select Notify Me. We send a one-time email when the variant is restocked. The notification is shipped within 30 minutes of stock arrival.

You can manage notifications in Account, then Notifications. Each request stays active for 90 days. After that the request is removed and you can sign up again.

## Why do products go out of stock?

Most variants go out of stock for one of three reasons:

- **High demand.** Promotions and seasonal launches sell faster than restocks arrive.
- **End of season.** Older seasons are not restocked once sold out.
- **Production pause.** A supplier may pause production to update materials or quality controls.

Where we have a confirmed restock date, the product page displays it under the Notify Me button.

## What does "limited edition" mean?

A limited edition product is produced in a single batch and is not restocked once sold out. The product page badge marks the item as Limited Edition and shows the production batch size. After sell-out the page remains available for reference, but the Notify Me button is hidden because no future stock is planned.

## What does "exclusive" mean?

An exclusive product is sold only on our storefront and is not available through other retailers. Exclusive items are restocked on the same schedule as standard products unless they are also marked Limited Edition.

## How accurate are product photos?

Photos use studio and on-model imagery to show silhouette, scale, and movement. Color reproduction is calibrated, but screen rendering varies. Where pattern placement varies between units, we note the variation in the description.

If a product arrives noticeably different from the photographs, return it under the standard return policy. Include a description of the difference and a photograph in the same lighting condition where possible.

## Where do I find product specifications?

The Specifications section of the product page lists materials, weight, country of origin, care instructions, and certifications where applicable. For technical apparel, additional fields include waterproof rating, breathability, and insulation weight.

If you need a specification not shown on the page, contact support and include the product link. We confirm with the supplier where needed and respond within one business day.

## How do I care for a product?

Read the care label sewn into the garment first; it overrides general guidance. The product page also lists key care steps, such as wash temperature, drying method, and ironing recommendations.

Following the care label preserves color, fit, and durability. Damage from improper care is not covered by the standard return policy.

## Are products sustainable?

Each product page lists relevant certifications, including organic cotton, recycled polyester, and fair-labor designations where applicable. Our Sustainability page summarizes our overall sourcing approach and program memberships.

If you have a specific sustainability question about a product, contact support with the product link. We respond within one business day.

## Can I see related accessories?

Most product pages include a Recommended For You section that lists complementary items. The recommendations are based on browsing patterns and editor curation. They are not affected by your account history beyond the current session.

## Questions

For questions about a specific product, contact support through the help center, include the product link and the variant attributes you are considering, and select the Product category. Our team responds within one business day.
