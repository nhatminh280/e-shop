# Order FAQ

This FAQ covers the most common questions about placing, tracking, changing, and cancelling an order. For payment-specific questions, see Payment Methods and Policy. For returns and refunds, see the Return and Refund Policy.

## How do I track my order?

Sign in, open Account, then Orders, and select the order you want to track. The order details page shows the current status, the carrier, the tracking number, and the most recent scan event. Select Track Package to open the carrier's tracking page in a new tab.

If you ordered as a guest, use the order lookup page with your order number and the email you used at checkout. The same details are available without signing in.

## What does each order status mean?

We use the following statuses, in the order an order typically moves through them:

- **PLACED.** Order received, payment in progress.
- **PAID.** Payment confirmed, order queued for fulfillment.
- **PROCESSING.** Warehouse is preparing the items for shipment.
- **SHIPPED.** Carrier has picked up the package; tracking is active.
- **OUT_FOR_DELIVERY.** Carrier is delivering the package today.
- **DELIVERED.** Carrier confirmed delivery.
- **CANCELLED.** Order was cancelled before shipment.
- **RETURNED.** Items were returned and accepted at the warehouse.
- **REFUNDED.** Refund has been issued to the original payment method.

If your order shows a status that is not in this list, contact support and include the order number.

## Can I change my order after placing it?

You can change shipping address, contact phone, and selected shipping method while the order is in `PLACED`, `PAID`, or early `PROCESSING`. Open the order and select Edit Order. Once an order moves to late `PROCESSING` or `SHIPPED`, self-service changes are not possible.

For changes to items or quantities, cancel the order before it ships and place a new one. Cancelling preserves any promotion that was applied at checkout, since the discount code remains valid until its expiry date.

## How do I cancel my order?

Open the order and select Cancel Order while the order is in `PLACED`, `PAID`, or early `PROCESSING`. Cancellation is immediate and a confirmation email is sent. The refund is initiated automatically based on the original payment method.

If the Cancel Order button is greyed out, the order has already moved past the cancellation window. In that case, refuse the delivery or use the standard return flow once you receive the package.

## My order is past the expected delivery date. What now?

Check the tracking page first. Carriers occasionally pause tracking updates for 24 to 48 hours during high-volume periods, even though the package is still moving.

If tracking has not updated for more than 48 hours, or if the expected delivery date has passed by more than 2 business days without delivery, contact support with the order number. We open a carrier investigation, which typically resolves within 5 to 10 business days.

## What if my order arrives incomplete?

Check the packing slip inside the package. We sometimes ship orders in multiple parcels, and another parcel with the missing items may arrive separately within 1 to 2 business days. The order details page shows each parcel and its tracking number.

If your order is confirmed as incomplete and no other parcel is on the way, contact support within 7 days of delivery. Include the order number and a list of missing items. We arrange a replacement or a refund at no cost.

## What if items in my order are damaged?

Take photographs of the outer packaging before opening if it is visibly damaged. After opening, photograph each affected item, the packing slip, and the inside of the box. Contact support within 7 days of delivery, include the photographs and order number, and we arrange a free return label and either a replacement or refund.

## I received the wrong item. What do I do?

Keep the wrong item in its original condition with tags attached. Contact support within 7 days of delivery, include the order number and a photograph of the item received. We arrange a free return label and ship the correct item at no cost, subject to availability.

## How do I download an invoice?

Open the order details page and select Download Invoice. The PDF includes order date, line items, taxes, and totals. To request a tax invoice with a company tax ID, see the Payment Methods and Policy.

## Can I add items to an existing order?

We cannot add items to an existing order because each order is treated as a single shipment unit. To add items, place a second order. We can sometimes consolidate two pending orders if they have the same address and have not yet entered processing; contact support immediately after placing the second order to request consolidation.

## Why was my order cancelled by the system?

Most automatic cancellations come from one of three causes:

- Payment did not complete within 24 hours of order placement.
- The shipping address could not be validated by the carrier.
- A fraud signal triggered a hold and we could not reach the customer within 72 hours.

Each automatic cancellation is followed by an email explaining the reason and the next step.

## Questions

For questions not answered here, contact support through the help center, sign in if possible, and select the Order category. Our team responds within one business day.
