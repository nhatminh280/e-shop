# Account FAQ

This FAQ answers the most common questions about creating, accessing, and managing a customer account on our storefront. For order-specific questions, see the Order FAQ. For payment questions, see the Payment Methods and Policy.

## How do I create an account?

Open the storefront and select Sign Up in the top navigation. Enter your email address, choose a password of at least 12 characters with mixed case and a digit, and confirm the email by clicking the verification link we send. Verification expires after 24 hours; if it does, request a new link from the sign-in page.

You can also create an account during checkout by ticking Save my details when entering your email. We use the same verification flow for those accounts.

## How do I sign in?

Open Sign In and enter your email and password. If you forgot your password, select Forgot Password and follow the email link to set a new one. Sign-in sessions stay active for 30 days unless you sign out manually or change your password.

## I did not receive the verification email. What now?

Check your spam folder first. Most missed emails sit there for 5 to 10 minutes before being filtered. If the email is still missing after 15 minutes, request a new verification link from the sign-in page. Each request invalidates the previous link.

If repeated requests do not arrive, contact support and include the email address you used. We confirm delivery status with our email provider and resend manually if needed.

## How do I reset my password?

Open Sign In and select Forgot Password. Enter your email address. We send a reset link valid for 30 minutes. Open the link, enter your new password twice, and submit. After a successful reset, sessions on all devices are signed out so you can sign in fresh.

If your reset link expires, request another from the same page. We do not let you reuse the previous five passwords.

## How do I update my profile?

Sign in, open Account, and select Profile. You can edit your name, phone number, default delivery address, and date of birth. Email changes require email verification on the new address before the update takes effect.

Profile updates apply to future orders only. They do not retroactively change addresses on orders that are already placed.

## How do I manage saved addresses?

Open Account, then Addresses. Add as many delivery addresses as you need. Set one as default for faster checkout. Delete or rename addresses at any time. Removing an address does not affect orders already shipped to it.

## How do I see my order history?

Open Account, then Orders. The list shows order number, date, status, and total. Select an order to see line items, payment status, shipment status, and tracking. Use the search box to find a specific order by number or product name.

Orders placed as a guest do not appear here. Use the order lookup page with the order number and the email you used at checkout.

## Can I merge two accounts?

We cannot merge accounts because order, address, and payment data are not interchangeable across accounts. The recommended approach is to keep using one account going forward. Mark the secondary account inactive by removing saved data and signing out.

## How do I sign out of all devices?

Open Account, then Security. Select Sign Out Everywhere. All active sessions are revoked immediately. Use this if you suspect your account is in use on a device you no longer control. After signing out everywhere, sign back in on your trusted devices.

## How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Open Account, then Security, then Two-Factor. Choose either an authenticator app or SMS. Confirm with a one-time code. Once enabled, sign-in requires both your password and a fresh code from your chosen method. Save the recovery codes shown during setup; you need them if you lose access to your authenticator or phone.

## What if I lose access to my two-factor method?

Use a recovery code from setup. If you do not have one, contact support with proof of identity, including the email on the account, the last order number, and a government-issued ID image with the photo masked. We disable two-factor within one business day after we verify the request.

## How do I delete my account?

Open Account, then Privacy, then Delete Account. Confirm with your password. We schedule deletion 14 days after the request to prevent accidental loss. During those 14 days, sign in to cancel the deletion. After the 14 days, the account, saved addresses, and saved payment methods are permanently removed. Order history is retained in compliance with tax law for 10 years, but is no longer linked to an active account.

## What data is kept after deletion?

For tax and accounting reasons, we retain order records, invoices, and refund records for 10 years. These records are stored without personal contact details where possible. We do not retain saved addresses, saved payment methods, or your account password.

## I forgot which email I used. What now?

Try the most likely emails first using the Forgot Password flow; we only send the reset email if the address is registered. If none works, contact support with your full name, the last order number you remember, and the approximate order date.

## Questions

For account questions not answered here, contact support through the help center, sign in if possible, and select the Account category. Our team responds within one business day.
